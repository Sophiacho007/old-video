Fourth Dimention  


 

10.11.2070


  Dear. Arthur

  I hope this letter can reach out to you safely.
  When you are opening this letter I am few days away to see you from another dimention.
  You might not remember me or remember me in different ways but I am your lover.
  In my dimension, you and I made lots of beautiful memories.
  We had dreams about building our own house together when our hair started getting white.
  When we talked about our dreams, you always said you will make a flying house so we can travel the world.
  It was such a silly dream but I always loved your smile and starry eyes when you talk about your dreams.
  Unfortunately, we could not make our dreams come true.
  I had to bury my love in the cold graveyard because of a tragic accident.
  My heart shattered apart like a broken glass at the night your body turned cold.
  I buried my memories and heart with you, Arthur.
  The next month that I went to put roses on your grave, I saw a poster about this clinical demonstration.
  It was about traveling dimensions to see people you lost and missed.
  I knew this was very risky but I had to throw myself into this.
  This is my fourth trial to reach out to you.
  I hope everything goes well and we can finally spend our life together.
  I hope we can find the way back home.
  See you soon.
  Xoxo.
  p.s. This is photo few month before the accident.
